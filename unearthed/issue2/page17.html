A couple of mighty Volvo L180H high lift loaders, named Kauri and Totara, went out to work at Juken New Zealand’s Kaitaia operation recently.

Juken are a New Zealand based company involved with forestry and wood producing. They are a key supplier of timber products to trade merchant chains throughout New Zealand and they have had an association with TransDiesel and Volvo equipment for 20-plus years.

The two purpose-built machines, assembled by our service team prior to delivery, will allow Juken the ability to stack product higher and load it faster.

The two beauties join the fleet of other new Volvo machinery from TransDiesel working at Juken’s Kaitaia Mill. This includes an EW240E MH Material Handler with grapple saw, EC220DL crawler excavator with grapple and an L180H wheel loader.
