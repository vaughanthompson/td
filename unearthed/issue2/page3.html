Welcome to Issue 2 of Unearthed!

Hi there. As of writing our New Zealand Olympics team have just finished the Tokyo games, reaching an incredibly impressive 13th spot in the medal rankings! It's apparent once again as a country we punch well above our weight- not just in sport but in business too.

For us here at TransDiesel our golden moment is providing 40 years of service to New Zealand industries and it's a podium position we're really proud to celebrate.

Coming together in a series of nationwide special events, it's been a privilege to share this achievement and tell a few yarns with customers old and new alike. Imagine what the 50th will be like!!

Enjoy this issue and stay warm and dry (if you're lucky enough to be in a cab!)
