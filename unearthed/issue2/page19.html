TransDiesel & Shell partner up with Hayden Paddon

Earlier this year we announced a new and exciting partnership between our Shell Lubricant division and Paddon Rallysport and the Hyundai New Zealand rally team.

Shell lubricants will be used in the Hyundai i20 AP4 throughout the 2021 NZ Rally Championship season.

According to Jason Steele our strong local heritage is a great fit with Hayden and Paddon Rallysport, as both brands are synonymous as being true ‘Kiwi Battlers’ - starting out small, putting in the hard yards and enjoying some great success along the way.

Our customer promise of – We’ve got your back – means that Hayden and the team can rest easy knowing that we are looking after them.

There are five rounds of the NZ Rally Championship throughout 2021. The first was the Rally of Otago from April 16 – 18th where there were 49 entries with Hayden the clear favourite. However it was Christchurch’s Dylan Thomson that claimed victory in the opening round.

Round two takes place in Whangarei from 14 – 16 May with further rounds scheduled for South Canterbury and Hawkes Bay before finishing in Coromandel.

The partnership will allow for some cross-promotional brand opportunities, social media collaboration as well as some exciting developments to coincide with TransDiesel’s 40th anniversary celebrations later in the year.

Update: Aug 2021

Champions!! Huge congratulations to Hayden and the team. With one round remaining (as of 1 Aug) Hayden and co-pilot John Kennard, have secured the NZ Rally Championship with a win at Hawkes Bay!
