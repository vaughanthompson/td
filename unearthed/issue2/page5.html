A new delivery service will provide bulk restocking of lubricants to key customers in the South Island. “We first debuted a Pump-Over Truck in the North Island about 12-months ago and, based on its popularity, we're now launching the service on the mainland.” said Jason Steele, TransDiesel Business Manager for Shell Lubricants.

“Utilising the Pump-Over Truck has enabled us to make significant operational and efficiency improvements as well as providing cost reductions for our customers.”

The vehicle can carry and pump from both Immediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) and barrels, as well as transport smaller packs loaded on pallets for transfer to less easily-accessible locations.

Capable of delivering consumables ranging from Shell Tellus S2 MX 22 light hydraulic fluids all the way through to heavy gear oils such as Shell Spirax S2 A 85W-140, the truck will also carry heavy-duty diesel Rimula engine oils for heavy-equipment users, and top-tier Helix passenger car oils for automotive garages and commercial fleet operators.

Customer tanks are connected to a remote monitoring system, ensuring lubricant never gets below a minimum level- providing the ability to operate to ‘just-in-time’ principles and order new product for delivery as and when required.

With both trucks in operation, we now have the ability to service the entire country with bulk oil and lubricant delivery.
