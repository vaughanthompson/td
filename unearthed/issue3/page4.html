Hautapu Hamilton Distribution Centre Opens

NEW TRANSDIESEL FACILITY CREATES JOBS, ENHANCES CUSTOMER SERVICE AND DELIVERS ON SUSTAINABILITY

TransDiesel has recently opened a brand new centrally-located national distribution centre.

Situated at 219 Peake Road, Hautapu, just south of Hamilton and positioned adjacent to the Waikato Expressway, the new facility will enhance the service offered to customers throughout the country.
“The new hub represents a significant investment in all-new infrastructure at a greenfields site and demonstrates our ongoing commitment to our customers,” said Mike McKessar, TransDiesel CEO.
