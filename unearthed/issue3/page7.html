Covid 19 wellness support program


In November TransDiesel employees commenced our COVID-19 Wellness Support Program. This program is all about looking after our people while we navigate the challenges of COVID-19.

As part of the program, employees were able to virtually attend a host of zoom meetings which covered range of activities, from financial wellbeing to budgeting and saving advice from BNZ, to an online self-led program about staying on track – getting through the tough times of stress. There was also be programs run by some external wellness specialists on everyday mindfulness, the power of resilience and stress management.
