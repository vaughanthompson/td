Kohler, the preferred power generation

Scott McAlpine, our Technical Support and Marine Products Manager in Auckland, is pleased to report on the sale and delivery of two Kohler 20.5EFKOZD to Whanganui based boat builders Q-West. And the order of two Kohler 80EFOZDJ to Yachting Developments, an award-winning builder of composite yachts in Auckland.


The Kohler 20.5EFKOZD will be used in Whale Watch Kaikōura’s next waka. You may remember, TransDiesel supplied two Kohler 20.5EFKOZD marine gensets on Whale Watch Kaikōura’s Waka Te Ao Marama last year. Te Ao Marama has been so successful for Whale Watch Kaikōura, that they have commissioned a sister boat.
