Taupō site update


Craig Wymer, Operations Manager – Central North Island, says despite the most recent lockdown and construction work not being able to take place until Alert Level 3, the Taupō site is coming along well.

The construction of the steel structure for the workshop started back in November. The office is now fully enclosed All things going to plan Craig says it is on track to open early February 2022 and the current Taupo team can’t wait to get their hands on an all new, purpose built facility.
