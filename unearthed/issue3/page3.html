Chief Executive, TransDiesel Ltd.

Welcome to Issue 3 of Unearthed!

What a year 2021 has been at TransDiesel! Our 40th year must have been one of our busiest and most challenging yet. While COVID continued to throw up a number of challenges there was so much to celebrate across our business some of which is detailed in our final newsletter for the year.

Our business is built on the foundations of our people, partners and of course loyal customers. I would personally like to thank all our customers for their support through the year.

Have a very Merry Christmas and a relaxing New Year. Be safe, create some memories with family and friends and we are looking forward to seeing everyone back in 2022.

Mike
