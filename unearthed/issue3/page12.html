Hayden Campbell

Welcome onboard to Hayden Campbell, National Aftersales Product & Business Development Manager based in Christchurch. Hayden brings a wealth of industry experience with him from the previous roles that he has held, Hayden is a qualified Equipment Technician, where he was on the tools for a number of years, he then moved into a Sales role, then back into Service Management.

Mick Skelton

In November we welcomed Mick Skelton to the team. Mick as Chief Information Officer (CIO) will be leading the IT functions and team he will also be a member of the Executive Leadership Team. Mick comes to us from Corys Electrical which is a Sonepar Company where Mick was CIO. Mick brings with him great experience from Corys which has 40 plus branches nationally and with a number of similarities to our business albeit in a different industry. Mick was also responsible for their Australian IT functions. With technology being one of our strategic pillars this is an extremely important area for the business's future, we look forward to Mick's contribution in helping us keep pace with the ever changing world of IT.
