Our Values

A commitment to Health & Safety

Health and Safety is a core goal for us. We will never compromise on the safety and wellbeing of our people. It is everyone’s job to ensure that all our people, customers and suppliers remain safe within our workplaces.

So, it was an eye opener last year at our leadership summit when legal expert in occupational health and Safety and partner at Chapman Tripp, Garth Galloway, asked why H & S was not part of our Vision and Values. A great question, and one we have quickly moved to answer. Health and Safety has now been incorporated into our value for passion.


PASSION: We love what we do. It shows in our positive and energetic attitude. The safety and wellbeing of our people is our highest priority.
