Rotorua New Location Open!

In January, our team at the TransDiesel Rotorua branch moved into their new home at 220 Te Ngae Road!

The brand new building creates an increased profile for the business as it’s situated just off the main road at 220 Te Ngae Road (about 1km down the road from our previous site)

Located in the heart of New Zealand’s forestry and wood processing industry, TransDiesel Rotorua is renowned for its service capability in transportation, forestry and related industries. Rotorua is also our main Allison and Detroit Diesel rebuild centre in the North Island.
