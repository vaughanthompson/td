Our new line-up of excavators offers class-leading features relating to power and efficiency, safety and innovation, as well as boasting European car-like levels of operator comfort.

Volvo is world-renowned as having outstanding safety credentials, with the same consideration extended from cars to heavy equipment.

“When it comes to excavators, no brand does it better. We’re confident the new Volvo E-Series range will find favour with owners and operators as it delivers significant on-site enhancements, most importantly less downtime, which means more opportunity to get the job done and add value to your business.” said Charles Malcolm, General Manager of Equipment, TransDiesel New Zealand.

A key enhancement of the E-Series is an engine upgrade to Stage 5 Emission compliance, which improves fuel efficiency, and highlights Volvo’s ‘high torque, at low RPM reputation. An upgraded hydraulics system raises pump power for smoother and faster operation, which results in quicker cycle times, higher productivity and lower operating costs.

A variety of working modes including idle, fine, general, heavy and power max are also available.

“Volvo’s vision in the environmental space with regards to sustainability aligns with our own company ideals. Our Volvo excavators are working in some remote and pristine locations around New Zealand across a range of different applications.”
