Message from Paul Harris

Chief Operating Officer, TransDiesel Ltd.

Despite several lockdowns over the past twelve months, TransDiesel is tracking well in terms of sales and growth.

We retained our position as number 1 Diesel engine supplier in New Zealand during the 2020 calendar year which means we have now held the number 1 position for 14 years in a row. The business also retained the number one position for marine generator sales for a fifth year. This success isn’t down to luck. It’s down to great people and great product and we are very fortunate to have both on the team.

Our construction equipment division also had a strong twelve months with demand for product continuing to grow. These are strong and encouraging results with the equipment market demonstrating growth after a few years of decline. TransDiesel retained their number 1 position for excavators, wheel loaders and articulated dump trucks when combined together. This is great news as it indicates not just growth for our business, but for all those other suppliers and contractors who do well when our sector does well.

It is now twelve months since we took over the distribution agreement for Shell Lubricants. After twelve years working with ENI oil products, it was

a big decision for us to shift partners and the decision has paid off, with sales exceeding initial expectations and all preliminary targets met. For me, this success is an example of how a business can shake up a sector simply by applying the principles of excellent service and caring about customer outcomes.

We didn’t set out to look for more business, but we did set out to deliver and expand on existing contractual arrangements, like cross selling of products and services. In addition, Shell Lubricants has one of the largest relationships as an Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) both locally and internationally, providing OEM supplies across several sectors.

The past twelve months haven’t been easy in this space. Like the rest of the country, we have grappled with supply chain issues and the TransDiesel team have really gone the extra mile to make sure we were able to meet expectations.
