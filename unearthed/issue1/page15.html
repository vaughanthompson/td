Shell making huge strides

In February we celebrated the 1-year anniversary of signing the distribution agreement with Shell and even though we have only been actively trading Shell for 10 months, it’s been a very eventful period.

Jason Steele, Business Manager – Shell Lubricants, says if you had told him a year ago, we would go through what we did and be where we are today, he would never have believed you!

“After 12 years working with ENI, it was a big decision to move away and start with something new, but a year on it’s clear it was the best thing we did. We are exceeding expectations and targets, and we are super excited about what lies ahead in 2021."
