Hydraulic fluid

Every part of your machine or process has been meticulously engineered to maximize performance -you should expect nothing less from your choice of lubricant. Our range of hydraulic fluids has been developed to enable equipment operators to select the oil that will deliver optimum value to their operations through enhanced wear protection, long oil life and high system efficiency.

Customer Success Stories




Australian Mine to switch to Shell Tellus after seeing 5.27% reduction in energy consumption during trial

During the trial, several factors were assessed including load, temperature, filters, filter ratings, lubricant viscosity and viscosity index. The energy consumption data was recorded by a highly accurate power monitor.

The results of the trial showed that using Shell Tellus S4 ME in the two hydraulic motors reduced their energy consumption by the equivalent of 5.27% per annum, which equates to NZD$20000 a year.

The company also benefited from extended oil-drain intervals and reduced labour costs and downtime. They also expect to extend equipment life.
